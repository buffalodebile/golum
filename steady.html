How it works

Balanced spreads its money across three building blocks: equities, gold and bonds. The aim is simple: capture most of the stock market's long-term growth while cutting the gut-wrenching drops, for a much smoother ride than a plain stock portfolio. The rules are fixed and rebalanced on a set schedule, with no discretion.

Diversification
Equities drive the growth, while gold and bonds tend to hold up when stocks wobble. Holding all three together smooths the ride and shrinks the deep drawdowns.
Low volatility by design
The blend is built to keep day-to-day swings and peak-to-trough losses small, so you can stay invested through rough patches instead of bailing at the bottom.
Smarter than a single basket
A plain stock portfolio is far more volatile for a similar long-run return. This mix is designed to beat US markets over time on a risk-adjusted basis: a similar destination, a calmer journey.

The exact mix and settings are the result of research and walk-forward validation, and are kept private.

Important disclaimers
