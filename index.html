The lower-risk side

What is quality investing?

Quality investing means owning the strongest businesses, not the flashiest tickers. Instead of chasing whatever is hot, it favours companies that are consistently profitable, carry little debt and grow steadily, then buys them at a reasonable price.

These are the kind of companies that tend to hold up better when markets fall and compound patiently over years, which is exactly why Quality sits on the calmer end of Prisma Capital's spectrum.

Profitable & growing

Consistent earnings and durable growth, not promises.

Financially solid

Low debt and strong balance sheets that survive bad years.

Sensibly priced

Great businesses only count when you don't overpay.
