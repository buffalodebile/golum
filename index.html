How it works

The strategy rotates between a leveraged Nasdaq-100 ETP and leveraged Gold, following simple, fixed rules. Signals are computed daily after the US close and executed automatically the next day, with no discretion.

Trend following
When the Nasdaq is in a medium-term uptrend, the portfolio holds leveraged Nasdaq, balanced with gold. When the trend breaks, it rotates fully into gold.
Volatility scaling
When market turbulence rises, Nasdaq exposure is automatically scaled down before trouble compounds.
Protective stop
A trailing stop on the whole portfolio cuts risk decisively in deep drawdowns.

The exact parameters are the result of years of research and walk-forward validation, and are kept private.

How it works

The strategy ranks US companies on quality, cash generation and reasonable valuation, then holds a focused, equal-weighted basket. The selection is recomputed twice a year from the latest published company accounts and rebuilt automatically, with no discretion.

Quality first
Only profitable, financially solid companies make the shortlist. Weak or deteriorating balance sheets are filtered out before anything else.
Cash flow & value
Among quality companies, the strategy favours those generating real cash and trading at a sensible price relative to that cash and their growth.
Focused & protected
The basket is concentrated and equal-weighted. A per-position protective stop limits the damage from any single holding between rebalances.

The exact selection rules are the result of years of research and walk-forward validation, and are kept private.
