How it works

The strategy rotates between a leveraged Nasdaq-100 ETP and leveraged Gold, following simple, fixed rules:

  • Trend following. When the Nasdaq is in a medium-term uptrend, the portfolio holds leveraged Nasdaq, balanced with gold. When the trend breaks, it rotates fully into gold.
  • Volatility scaling. When market turbulence rises, Nasdaq exposure is automatically scaled down before trouble compounds.
  • Protective stop. A trailing stop on the whole portfolio cuts risk decisively in deep drawdowns.
  • No discretion. Signals are computed daily after the US close and executed automatically the next day. No forecasts, no news-reading, no gut feelings.

The exact parameters are the result of years of research and walk-forward validation, and are not disclosed.

Important disclaimers

Backtest vs live. The dimmed curve is a simulation on historical index data with cost assumptions. It is a model, not a track record. Live results (colored) differ from the model and will keep differing.
